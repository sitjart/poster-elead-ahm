The pattern: women coordinate. Men still hold most seats with budgets, strategy and technical authority. 2022/24 are estimates from published charts; 2026 reflect the headcounts on ELIXIR's site. Deputies for Technical/Node/Training Coordinator roles are not counted.

Diverse teams outperform – but only when paired with psychological safety.

  • The business case is settled. McKinsey (2023) and the British Science Association (2025): inclusive leadership drives innovation, retention and resilience across sectors and STEM.
  • The academic case is documented. OECD (2024) and peer-reviewed analyses show persistent gaps in research career pathways, mobility and senior representation – despite parity at entry.
  • Diversity without safety underperforms. Edmondson (Harvard Business Review, 2022) and Google's Project Aristotle: the highest-performing teams pair difference with the freedom to speak up.

How ELEAD works: three pillars – 1:1 mentoring with senior leaders, peer mentoring in small confidential groups, and professional training delivered by external experts.

WHAT WE MEASURE

Four impact areas.

ELEAD tracks impact across four interlinked areas. Each case on the left carries a score from 1 to 3: indirect · clear benefit · direct impact.

  1. Human Capital. Leadership pipeline strengthened; talent retained inside ELIXIR.
  2. Relationship Capital. Cross-Node collaboration; mentoring and peer networks build trust.
  3. Resource Uptake & Legacy. Reflective leadership shifts culture; ELEAD's model adopted elsewhere.
  4. Research Efficiency. Better workflows and decisions; leadership development supports sustainability.

Where ELEAD goes next

With internal funding closed, ELEAD's future depends on adoption.

ACROSS INFRASTRUCTURES

Do we want to lead – or follow?

ELIXIR has built one of the most hands-on, output-driven EDI leadership programmes in European research infrastructures. Several peer RIs have looked at us as a model to replicate.

They have done less hands-on intervention but more publishing – strong gender-equality and EDI policies. We have remained quiet about our own wins.

ELEAD has measurable outcomes, four impact case studies, a logic model and an external Scientific Advisory Board, and active conversations are open with other RIs and Pistoia Alliance.

INSIDE NODES

Take it home.

ELEAD doesn't need to be run centrally to continue. It can return as your Node's programme. Materials are open; the co-leads encourage adaptation.

  • ELIXIR-UK – won the PeerLadder national grant; ELEAD-based peer-mentoring for under-represented dRTPs is running now. See the PeerLadder poster.
  • EMBL-EBI – Mallory Freeberg's ad-hoc team peer-mentoring group is live and ready to scale out.
  • ELIXIR-GR / CH / ES – national spin-offs explored at AHM 2025
